Who We Are

LedAffinity is an architectural lighting company at the forefront of experience-driven LED technology.

Public places, monuments and façades shape the face of a city at night with their illumination. Lighting is much more than just functional tasks – it also determines the character and identity of public buildings and areas. It brings recognition, orientation and illuminates iconic spaces. In today’s multicultural cities the language of light is cross-cultural and experiential. LedAffinity lighting is designed to refine the urban space with exceptional lighting that is cost-efficient and creates livable cities.

What We Do

We have everything you need to highlight your landmark and enhance the visitor experience.

Good city planning and architecture reflects the needs and aspirations of a society. Creating new features or maintaining existing structures provides both opportunities and responsibilities for planners and designers. With meaningful design and quality light, it can help a space to become qualitative cultural asset in the city.

Cove

LedAffinity Cove is a cove lighting system. Compatible with DMX and ideal for cove applications with high quality of light is a requirement.

Pixel Bar

LedAffinity Pixel Bar is an outdoor rated, pixel controllable, direct view LED fixture. Featuring RGBW it is ideal for any interior or exterior application. It is compatible with DMX.

Flex Tape

LedAffinity Flex Tape is a flexible lighting system that allows you to shape with light. Available in custom lenghts and LED configurations it is a versatile solution for architectural applications.

Power

For consistent and long-lasting performance, LedAffinity recommends to use high-end power supply units in combination with lighting controls and lighting fixtures.

Controls

At LedAffinity we enable our clients to communicate with our fixtures via the universal DMX 512 protocol. Clients are able to use their own control system or use any of our compatible systems.

Technology

Technology is an enabler in achieving the architect and designer's vision. We give clients access to custom made LEDs, custom lenghts, precision optics, accurate pixel control and large scalability with our control protocols.
